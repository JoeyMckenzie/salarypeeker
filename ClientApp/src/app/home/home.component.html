Salary Peeker

What is Salary Peeker?

Salary Peeker is a consolidation of publically available salary data for both the City of Redding and Shasta County, with the purpose of providing the denizens of Redding a platform for transparency into the designation of budgeted salaries for both agencies. Salary Peeker is an analogous project to Budget Peeker, an open source application that provides financial data for the the City of Sacramento's approved budget from fiscal years 2015 to 2018.

The Data?

Salary Peeker sources its data from Transparent California, an open data portal for salary information of the public sector within the state of California. Data is sorted by various pay types, agencies, fiscal year, and job titles.
